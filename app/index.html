Spolia Wall

A 20m × 1m architectural installation composed of hundreds of salvaged objects from European marketplaces, stacked across three balcony railings.

An AI procurement agent (Antoine) continuously scouts, negotiates, and purchases physical materials — each appearing on this 3D wall in real time.

Click any element to see its full provenance story. Objects with 3D meshes show their original textures; others appear as coloured blocks indicating their pipeline status.
