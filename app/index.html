Spolia Wall

Three 20-metre balcony railings filled with hundreds of salvaged objects — stone, ceramic, metal, glass, and found materials sourced from European marketplaces.

An AI procurement agent (Antoine) autonomously scouts, evaluates, negotiates, and purchases physical materials. Each acquisition appears here in real time as a 3D object on the wall.

The architect reviews and approves objects before they are allocated to specific wall positions. Five composition rules govern placement: material adjacency, depth variation, and fire zone compliance.

How to use:

  • Click any object to see its provenance, negotiation log, dimensions, and status
  • Double-click to fly in close and inspect
  • Scroll to zoom, right-drag to pan, left-drag to orbit
  • Use the toolbar below or keyboard shortcuts to toggle overlays
